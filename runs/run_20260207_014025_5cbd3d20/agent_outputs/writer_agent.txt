Released in 2019, _Jana Nayagan_ tells the story of a person who is aware of the truth, known as "jana," and his journey to uncover the secrets behind a mysterious snake-like creature known as "_nayagan._" The film stars Vijay, one of Tamil cinema's most prominent actors, in a role that showcases his versatility and acting prowess. With a well-crafted narrative, impressive action sequences, and stunning cinematography, _Jana Nayagan_ has left a lasting impact on the industry.

* **Box Office Performance:** _Jana Nayagan_ grossed ₹35 crores at the Indian box office, with a worldwide total of ₹60 crores.
